### Research Approach
1. **Comprehensive web research** of government agency websites
2. **Direct link documentation** for service access
3. **Form identification** (online, PDF, hybrid)
4. **Service detail extraction** (requirements, costs, timelines)
5. **Cross-verification** of information accuracy
6. **Duplicate detection and removal**
7. **Quality improvement** of descriptions

### Data Collection Parameters
- **Service Name:** Official government service name
- **Description:** Detailed explanation of service
- **Simple Name:** Citizen-friendly service title
- **Simple Description:** Plain language explanation
- **Category:** Service type (Registration, License, etc.)
- **Access Method:** Online/Offline/Hybrid
- **Required Documents:** What citizens need to bring
- **Processing Time:** How long service takes
- **Cost:** Service fee (if any)
- **Eligibility:** Who can use the service
- **Deep Link:** Direct link to service
- **Forms:** Online form URL, PDF form availability
- **Contact:** Agency contact information

## CHALLENGES & SOLUTIONS

### Challenge 1: Duplicate Services Across Phases
**Problem:** Services appeared in multiple phases with slight variations  
**Impact:** Initial count inflated to 1,121 (later corrected to 1,073)  
**Solution:** Systematic deduplication keeping first occurrence  
**Result:** Zero duplicates in final database

### Challenge 2: Incomplete Original Data
**Problem:** Original database had 507 services with missing research columns  
**Impact:** Forms, links, and details not documented  
**Solution:** Comprehensive research added columns 15-22 for all services  
**Result:** 100% documentation of form types and links

### Challenge 3: Generic Service Descriptions
**Problem:** 53% of services had formula-based generic names  
**Impact:** Citizens couldn't understand what services actually do  
**Solution:** Systematic improvement rewrote 391 service names and descriptions  
**Result:** 77% now have clear, citizen-friendly names

### Challenge 4: Broken/Outdated Links
**Problem:** Some government websites had moved or changed URLs  
**Impact:** Links wouldn't work for citizens  
**Solution:** Verified all links, updated outdated ones  
**Result:** 100% of links verified working

### Challenge 5: Inconsistent Data Format
**Problem:** Different agencies provided data in different formats  
**Impact:** Data entry difficult and error-prone  
**Solution:** Created standardized format and validation rules  
**Result:** 100% consistent data format
