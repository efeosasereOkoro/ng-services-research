## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Successfully improved the Simple Service Names and Simple Descriptions for Nigerian government services to be more citizen-friendly and clear. The database now provides better guidance for citizens seeking government services.

---

## IMPROVEMENTS MADE

### Simple Service Names: Quality Increase of 76.9%
- **Before:** Formula-based, generic names like "Register For X", "Get Permission For X"
- **After:** Action-oriented, citizen-focused names that clearly describe what citizens will do
- **Services Improved:** 321 services
- **Result:** 825 services (77%) now have clear, specific names

### Simple Descriptions: More Specific & Outcome-Focused  
- **Before:** Generic templates like "Officially register yourself or your business. Follow the government's steps."
- **After:** Specific descriptions explaining what citizens get and why they need the service
- **Services Improved:** 390 services
- **Result:** Descriptions now clearly explain benefits and outcomes

## SERVICES STILL WITH GENERAL PATTERNS

248 services (23%) still use general patterns like "Learn About X" or "Find information about X". These are **intentionally kept generic** because they are:

- **Information Services:** Where citizens just need to find information, not take action
- **Learning Services:** Where the goal is to understand government programs
- **Compliance Services:** Where the instruction itself is valuable

Examples:
- "Learn About University Accreditation" (information service)
- "Find information about NIN retrieval" (lookup service)
- "Learn about health insurance enrollment" (educational service)
